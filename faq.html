Child Vaccination FAQ

What vaccines are recommended for children?

Recommended vaccines for children include measles, mumps, rubella (MMR), polio, hepatitis B, and many others. Consult with your healthcare provider for a complete schedule.

When should my child receive vaccinations?

Vaccination schedules vary, but most children receive vaccines starting at birth and continue throughout childhood. Your pediatrician will provide a vaccination schedule.

Are vaccines safe for my child?

Yes, vaccines are rigorously tested for safety and effectiveness before being approved for use. The benefits of vaccination far outweigh the risks.

Do vaccines cause autism?

No, extensive research has shown no link between vaccines and autism. Vaccines are essential for preventing serious diseases.
